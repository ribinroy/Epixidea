What's The Process?

Our process is simple and straight-forward –

  • Select your preferred video package; this includes the style and quality that suits your needs.
  • Fill out a questionnaire with the important details of your product.
  • Ship your product to our studio and once we receive it, your video will be completed within 10 – 15 business days.

What’s the delivery time?

Delivery time tends to vary based on when we receive your product. If you’re shipping your product from Amazon or eBay, then you can expect it 15 days after we receive it.

What if I need additional actors?

You can add a single actor to your video for $150 However, if you need several actors, please reach out to us to create a custom invoice for you.

What’s your revision policy?

Given that we hold customer satisfaction highly, we offer unlimited revisions. This means that we’ll work with you until you’re satisfied with the final video. Please, when requesting a revision, endeavor to include viable info to guide our editing.
